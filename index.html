Child Art Therapy Program

Information

Location

Oriole Community Centre 2975 Don Mills Rd W, North York, ON.
Dates

June 1st -18th from 9a.m-5p.m June 19th-25th from 9 a.m- 5p.m (2 separate workshops)
For Who?

Children aged 2-10 with mental health impairments.
Mission

To help children alleviate negative mental health symptoms through art and allow them to build emotional regulation and positive coping skills.

Values

We value a holistic approach to teaching children the benefits of art therapy to foster positive mental health, emotional skills, and fine motor skills.

Workshops Offered

Talk Therapy Workshop

Get a chance to talk with your therapist about your mental health issues to create proper coping mechanisms.

Read more
Art Therapy Workshop

Children will have the opportunity to engage in several art creativities, such as painting, colouring, drawing, mask making, etc., to alleviate anxiety, depression and stress the children may be feeling.
